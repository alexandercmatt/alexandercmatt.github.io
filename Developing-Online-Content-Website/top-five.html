Alexander's Top Five Videos

These videos have all consumed my life at one point or another.

  • Sideways - Why the Music in Cats (2019) is Worse than you Thought
    The editing in this video is just hysterical, and it taught me to appreciate Cats the musical, which is an impressive feat.
  • Johnstone - I Caught Every Shiny Sinnoh Pokemon
    I have watched this video truly an ungodly amount of times.
  • WolfeyVGC - How I Won the Largest Pokemon Tournament Ever (Again)
    Absolutely banger video, I never knew Pokemon could be this hype.
  • Moxie2D - 100 Days in a Pokémon Nuzlocke: The Anime
    Introduced me to my favourite song ever and made Pokemon Scarlet feel incredibly hype.
  • Defunctland - Disney Channel's Theme: A History Mystery
    A passionate letter to unappreciated art and a beautiful tribute to a late artist. We are what we leave behind.
